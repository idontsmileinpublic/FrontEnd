The How To Art restaurant is a great example of combining art and food in one place. The craving for literature and paintings is read in everything - from design to literary cocktails in a bar on the third floor of the institution. The essence of our menu lies in the tastes and traditions of Ukrainian and European cuisines and cultures. It is a broad and rich spectrum of influences that are unified by fresh produce and full flavours that harmoniously coalesce.
